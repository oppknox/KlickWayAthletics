ABOUT KLICKWAY ATHLETICS

We're more than just a gym. We're a community dedicated to helping you achieve your fitness goals and transform your life.

OUR STORY

FROM PASSION TO PURPOSE

KlickWay Athletics was founded by Brian Klick in 2010 as a professional alternative to cookie-cutter workouts, corporate gym trainers, and fad diets. With years of professional training and competition experience, Brian understood that there is no one-size-fits-all plan that works for everyone.

What started as a small personal training studio in Seattle has grown into multiple locations throughout the area, each maintaining the personalized approach and community feel that sets KlickWay apart from other gyms.

Today, KlickWay Athletics continues to evolve and expand, but our core mission remains the same: to provide personalized fitness solutions that deliver real results for real people.

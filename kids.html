Welcome to KlickWay Kids Club

Our Kids Club is designed to give parents peace of mind while they train. Kids ages 12 months to 12 years old can enjoy a safe, fun environment supervised by friendly staff trained in CPR, AED, and First Aid.

Children will have plenty to do, from age-appropriate games and play areas to arts, crafts, and safe activity zones. For kids a little older, we also offer Kid Fit classes (ages 7–12) that build healthy habits through fun movement and activities.

KlickWay Kids Club Policies

At KlickWay Athletics, our Kids Club is a supervised play area designed to give parents peace of mind while they work out. Please note: Kids Club is not a licensed daycare facility. Parents/guardians must remain on gym premises at all times.

General Guidelines

  • Age Range: 1 year to 12 years
  • Maximum stay: 2 hours per visit
  • Children 13+ may use workout floor with supervision
  • Must be on active family membership
  • No friends, cousins, or babysitting arrangements

Check-In/Out

  • Only parent/guardian on membership can check in/out
  • Children must be on family membership
  • Valid membership required for entry
  • Sign-in/out required each visit

Health & Safety

  • No sick children (fever, cough, rash, etc.)
  • Staff do not provide hygiene assistance
  • Parents paged if assistance needed
  • No food allowed; drinks in spill-proof containers
  • Socks or shoes required at all times
  • No wet clothing or swimsuits

Behavior Expectations

  • Parents called if child cries >10 minutes
  • Time-outs used for minor issues
  • Unsafe behavior may result in suspension
  • Personal toys not allowed
  • Electronics permitted for ages 5+ (must be labeled)
  • KlickWay not responsible for lost/damaged items

Important Notes

  • Entry may be limited if Kids Club reaches capacity
  • KlickWay reserves the right to refuse care if behavior poses risk
  • This is a supervised play area, not a licensed daycare
  • Parents must remain on premises at all times

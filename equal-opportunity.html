Our Commitment to Equal Opportunity

KlickWay Athletics is proud to be an Equal Opportunity Employer

KlickWay Athletics is committed to providing equal employment opportunities to all qualified individuals without regard to race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, age, disability, genetic information, veteran status, or any other legally protected characteristic.

We believe that diversity strengthens our organization and enriches our workplace culture. Our commitment to equal opportunity extends to all aspects of employment, including recruitment, hiring, promotion, compensation, benefits, training, and termination.

Reasonable Accommodations

Supporting all team members to succeed

Disability Accommodations

KlickWay Athletics provides reasonable accommodations to qualified individuals with disabilities during the application process and throughout employment. We are committed to ensuring that our workplace is accessible to all team members.

If you need an accommodation during the application process or to perform the essential functions of your job, please contact our HR department at hr@klickwayathletics.com.

Religious Accommodations

We respect the religious beliefs and practices of all team members. KlickWay Athletics will make reasonable accommodations for religious observances, practices, and beliefs, provided they do not create an undue hardship on business operations.

Reporting Discrimination or Harassment

We take all reports seriously and investigate promptly

1

Report Immediately

Report any incidents of discrimination, harassment, or retaliation to your supervisor, HR, or management.

2

Thorough Investigation

We will conduct a prompt, thorough, and impartial investigation of all reported incidents.

3

Appropriate Action

We will take appropriate corrective action if violations of our policies are found.

4

No Retaliation

We prohibit retaliation against anyone who reports concerns or participates in an investigation.
